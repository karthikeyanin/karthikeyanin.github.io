Blog Submission to Search Engines



Next thing is the Search Engine Optimization, In order to reach the event based blog is let the Search Engines know about your blog.

How to submit website to Search Engines?

Sign in using Google webmasters , Add your website and verify using any of the simple method and Once you are in the Console, Under Sitemap options, Add the below URL

(Your full site url)/sitemap.xml

Click submit, It will verify and submission will happen automatically, Every links and pages on your blog will be crawled by google search engine and make available for viewers.

You can try alternatives such as
  • Bing webmasters
  • Yandex tools

Keyword Planning

Another factor is Keywords, Using the top long keywords related to the article and making the Meta tags, Meta keywords will help you to gain more audience, For that create more tags for the article and use hash tags while sharing the posts in social media to gain the article visible to that specific keywords.
  • Use Long Tail Keywords
  • Use Keyword Planner to find long Tail Keywords
  • Do not build more back links rather build quality back links
  • Use Keywords in H1,H2,H3 and in Meta Description
